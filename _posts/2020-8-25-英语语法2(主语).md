# 主语
## 1.主语的成份
名词,代词,非谓,从句(引导词+句子)
* 例:
Handsome and strong are his nature.
错,形容词不可做主语,可改为:
Handsomess and strength are his nature, 或:
Being handsome and strong is his nature. 使用Be将原句主语变为非谓,
## 2. 主语能不能少?不能.
* 没有主语怎么办?
  * 1. 使用it,与天气,时间,温度有关时.
    * it is raining.雨一直下
    * it fells exceedingly hot in cabin
  * 2. there be结构,'有'.
    * there exist(seem,remain) a host of undergraduates being facinated with me.(要非谓)
  * 3. 被动,无主语工人称代词作主,可用被动
    * persistence must be pointed out outstandly crucial
    * Overfishing is claimed more than fearful by an increasing amount of profesors.
  * 4. 人称代词(尽量不用)
    * If there exists a dream, glories are supposed to be achieved.
