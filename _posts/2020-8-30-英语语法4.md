# 并列句
1. 定义:就是用连词连接的两个句子
I love you, but you love that dog.
Although I love you, you love that dog.

2. 常见的连词
* 平行
  * 连词: and, not only..but also, either .. or, neigther..nor, both..and
  * 副介: similarly, equally, likewise, at the same time, in the meanwhile
* 转折
  * 连词:but,yet, while, whereas
  * 副介:however, nevertheless, on the contrary, conversely, unexpectedly, unfortunately, by contrast.
* 选择
  * 连词:or, whether..or...
  * 副词: alternatively
* 因果
  * 连词:for, so
  * 副词:therefore, thus, consequently, as a result
* 递进
  * 连词:then
  * 副词:besides, furthermore, moreover, additionally, subsequently, in addition

写作:上下句间有逻辑关系同定要用逻辑关系词:连,副,介,介短
Romance has evaporated, **but** she still misses the past.
这个but是连词,它前面可以是逗号,也可以是没有.若将but换成介短:on the contrary,则前面必须为句号,即:Romance has evaporated. On the contrary she still misses the past.因为介短不能连接两个句子,因此前面要国句号.
若即想用介短,又想用逗号,可改成:Romance has evaporated, and on the contrary she still miss the past.
因为and是唯一没有意义的连词.

* 连与副,介短的区别:连前有无逗号均可,其他要没用句号分成两句话,要么加连词and.
